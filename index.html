Smoked Oak

Smoked Oak cabinets undergo a special treatment where the wood is exposed to smoke or heat, resulting in a darker and richer color. This process enhances the natural grain patterns of the oak, creating a unique and visually appealing appearance.

Available in Plywood & Particle boards.

Blue Single Shaker

Blue cabinets can add a bold and vibrant element to your kitchen, creating a focal point and injecting personality into the space. The color blue is versatile and can be chosen in various shades, ranging from soft pastels to deep navy or even bright turquoise, allowing you to tailor the look to your personal style and the overall design scheme of your kitchen.

Blue single shaker kitchen cabinets can be constructed from a variety of materials, including solid wood or medium-density fiberboard (MDF). Solid wood cabinets provide a durable and high-end option, while MDF cabinets are more cost-effective and resistant to warping or cracking.

Available in Plywood & Particle boards.

Natural Wood

Natural wood cabinets can be customized to suit your specific preferences and needs. They can be designed with various door styles, including raised panel, recessed panel, or shaker, allowing you to achieve the desired level of detail and sophistication.

Available in Plywood & Particle boards.

White High Gloss

White high gloss cabinets create a clean and modern appearance in the kitchen. The high gloss finish reflects light, giving the cabinets a shiny and reflective surface that adds a sense of brightness and openness to the space.

Available in Plywood & Particle boards.

White Slim Shaker

White slim shaker kitchen cabinets combine a contemporary aesthetic with the timeless elegance of shaker design. They provide a versatile and stylish option for homeowners looking to create a modern and functional kitchen space.

White slim shaker cabinets are typically constructed from high-density fiberboard (HDF) with a painted white finish. Solid wood cabinets offer durability and a natural aesthetic, while HDF cabinets are more affordable and resistant to warping or cracking.

Available in Plywood & Particle boards

Grey Single Shaker

Gray Single Shaker is a timeless color that can withstand changing trends. It offers longevity in your kitchen design, ensuring that your cabinets remain stylish for years to come.

Oppein Cabinetry offers Lighter shades of gray cabinets have the advantage of reflecting light, helping to create a brighter and more spacious feel in the kitchen. This is especially beneficial for smaller or darker kitchen spaces.

Available in Plywood & Particle boards.
